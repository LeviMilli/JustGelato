So Much More Than Icecream

It's unlike any frozen treat you've had.

Gelato is a traditional Italian frozen dessert made with fresh milk, cream, and sugar, and flavored with fruit, nuts, chocolate, and other natural ingredients. It is churned at a slower speed than ice cream, which results in a denser and creamier texture. Gelato also contains less fat and air than ice cream, making it more flavorful and rich.

In addition, gelato is typically served at a slightly warmer temperature than ice cream, which allows the flavors to fully develop on the palate. All of these factors make gelato a superior choice for anyone looking to indulge in a truly delicious and satisfying frozen treat.
